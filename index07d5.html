Hello and welcome!


Through the following internet pages you will gain insight into my life and work.

For quite a few years now, I am passionate about pottery in general and particularly about creating salt glazed stoneware fired in a gas kiln at 1280°C (2350 °F). Since the moment I decided to learn the trade of a potter, I belong to the species of people who were able to make their hobby to a profession.

Searching for my own, individual pottery stile, I have been experimenting with different clays and glazes. Thus my little stoneware selection is constantly subject to change. By modifying smaller or bigger details, I am trying to improve the form, look and function of my products.

In general, the individual, handmade pieces, which are created on a potter’s wheel and attached with three-dimensional decorations, are unique.

To obtain hygienic and even surfaces, which are easy to clean, the inner side of the vessels is coated with nontoxic and food-safe glazes.
