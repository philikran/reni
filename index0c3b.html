Offered Courses at Public Schools


At the moment, I am offering pottery courses for children in the framework of all-day schooling opportunities at two public schools, which are the “Oberland” grammar school in Seifhennersdorf and the “Albert Schweizer” school centre for children with learning difficulties in Ebersbach.

Within these courses, the children learn some simple pottery techniques. Subsequently, they create their own pinch, coil or slab pots. Dealing with the clay, motor skills are trained and the children learn how to transform their ideas in a subtle way and to choose the right proportions. After that, they can paint their works with coloured engobes and after letting them dry and firing them they can take their own creations home with them.
