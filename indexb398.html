Personal Background

Irene Wenzel
  • born in 1983
  • secondary school certificate in 2000
  • 2001 first job experiences with Iris Schöne, Großhennersdorf
  • “Abitur” certificate in 2003
  • 2003 The wish to make my hobby to a profession assumes shape. In connection with applying for an apprenticeship position I made an internship at the pottery works of Jens-Peter Planke in Lenzen, Hans Grünert in Waldenburg and Thomas Thunig in Schmölln (Saxony)
  • 2003-2006 Apprenticeship in the pottery works of Thomas Thunig in Schmölln, theoretic education at the trade school “Berufliche Schule Porzellan, Meißen” in Meißen
  • 2006 I finish my apprenticeship with the apprenticeship diploma; 1. place among the ceramists/potters in the competition “Praktischer Leistungswettbewerb der Handwerksjugend 2006“ of the federal state Saxony and 3. place in the same-titled national competition; 1. place in the competition “Die Gute Form, Handwerker gestalten” in Saxony and 3. place in the same-titled national competition
  • 2006-2007 potter at the works of Thomas Thunig
  • 2007 courses and internships, e.g. at the works of Michael Sälzer in Kaub (woodfiring), Peter Dolacinski in Götemitz and Mechthild Kießling in Radebeul
  • since July 2007 potter at the works of Mechthild Kießling
